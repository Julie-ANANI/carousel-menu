Typography

Typography sets default styles for headings, paragraphs, blockquote and lists elements.

Headings

H1 Heading (2rem = 40px)

H2 Heading (1.6rem = 32px)

H3 Heading (1.4rem = 28px)

H4 Heading (1.2rem = 24px)

H5 Heading (1rem = 20px)
H6 Heading (0.8rem = 16px)

        <h1>H1 Heading</h1>
        <h2>H2 Heading</h2>

Paragraphs

The space after the paragraph is of 0.4rem i.e. 8px.


        <p class="text-md">The space after the paragraph is of 0.4rem i.e. 8px.</p>
        <p>para 2</p>

Blockquote

The advance of technology is based on making it fit in so that you don't really even notice it, so it's part of everyday life.

- Bill Gates 

        <blockquote>
          <p>The advance of technology is based on making it fit in so that you don't really even notice it, so it's part of everyday life. </p>
          <cite>- Bill Gates</cite>
        </blockquote>
