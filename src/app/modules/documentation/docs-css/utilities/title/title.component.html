Title

Simple headings to add depth to the page.

There are 2 types of heading: title .title and subtitle .subtitle

title

subtitle


        <h1 class="title">title</h1>
        <h2 class="subtitle">subtitle</h2>

        <h3 class="subtitle is-3">Subtitle 3</h3>
        <h4 class="subtitle is-4">Subtitle 4</h4>
        <h5 class="subtitle is-5">Subtitle 5 (default size)</h5>
        <h6 class="subtitle is-6">Subtitle 6</h6>

When you combine a title and a subtitle, they move closer together. You can maintain the normal spacing between titles and subtitles if you use the is-spaced class on the first element.

Title 1

Subtitle 3


        <p class="title is-1 is-spaced">Title 1</p>
        <p class="subtitle is-3">Subtitle 3</p>
