Typography

Typography sets default styles for headings, paragraphs, blockquote and lists elements.

Headings

Simple headings to add depth to the page.

H1 Heading (40px)

H2 Heading (32px)

H3 Heading (28px)

H4 Heading (24px)

H5 Heading (20px)
H6 Heading (16px)

        <h1>H1 Heading</h1>
        <h2>H2 Heading</h2>
        <h3>H3 Heading</h3>
        <h4>H4 Heading</h4>
        <h5>H5 Heading</h5>
        <h6>H6 Heading</h6>

Paragraphs

The space after the paragraph is 8px.


        <p>The space after the paragraph is 8px.</p>

Blockquote

The advance of technology is based on making it fit in so that you don't really even notice it, so it's part of everyday life.

- Bill Gates 

        <blockquote>
          <p>The advance of technology is based on making it fit in so that you don't really even notice it, so it's part of everyday life. </p>
          <cite>- Bill Gates</cite>
        </blockquote>
