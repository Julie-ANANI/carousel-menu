Close

A versatile close cross. The close element is a stand-alone element that can be used in different contexts. On its own, it's a simple circle with a cross. When you hover over it, it shows the overlay background. 


        <div class="close"></div>

To show the cross icon with the background use the has-background class with the close class.


        <div class="close has-background"></div>

To show the cross icon of the white color on the dark background use is-white class and you can also use has-hover-white to show the white hover background with the close class.


        <div class="close has-background"></div>

Sizes

It comes in 4 different sizes.


        <div class="close is-background is-xs"></div>
        <div class="close is-background is-sm"></div>
        <div class="close is-background is-normal"></div>
        <div class="close is-background is-lg"></div>
